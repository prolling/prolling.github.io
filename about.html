Clear Lake Connected

During the summer of 2020, one of my friends and I saw an issue in our small town. We saw that many small businesses were struggling to market themselves online. With COVID-19, it was more important than ever for them to market themselves, and we saw a way that we could help them. We created our business, Clear Lake Connected, to be a social media marketing provider for these businesses. Since our start, we have made over $7000 in profit, and much more through winning the 2020 NIACC YEA pitch competition, and getting through the qualifying round for the 2021 NIACC College Pitch off compeition. Iowa State WiSE

WiSE, or Women in Science and Engineering, is a program at Iowa State that supports women in STEM majors by inspiring, supporting, and empowering current and future students to persist in their STEM majors. During the spring 2022 semester, I worked as the social media intern and as an SRM. As the social media intern, I generated, created, and pushed out social media content as well as working with a variety of teams. As an SRM, or Student Role Model, I traveled to various schools/events around Iowa to do activities with kids there with the intention of facilitating a love of STEM. Although I do not currently work in the WiSE office, I am still a very active participant in the program.
